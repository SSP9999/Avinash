Our Story

The roots of Avinassh took place when Founder Pragnanand has started “Global AID”, Non-Government Organisation along with his wife Sai Padma in 2008 which operates in the remote tribal and rural areas of Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram and Srikakulam districts of Andhra Pradesh. Looking at his wife who is a polio survivor and gross scoliosis, a deep thought stuck in the mind of Pragnanand to try alternative healing methods.

  • Realising that Yoga, the ancient Indian practice which focuses on the entire well being can provide some answers, he has completed Post-graduate program in Yoga from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. His mission did not stop there.
  • With an intent to spread his learnings he has tried his finger into the research of ancient & traditional foods and thus formed AVINASHH (Adiseshu Vanajakshi Institute of Natural & Alternative Systems for Health & Healing) in 2014. The journey to take healthy food to the plates of millions at an affordable cost has commenced.
  • The journey has led to four years of meticulous research to innovate millet flour that eases the process to make rotis simple and efficient. Incorporating millet diet into their lifestyle Pragnanand and his wife have gradually reduced medication. He manages type-2 diabetes through millet diet and had good results. He has tried similar experiments with close family members and extended families.

Currently, in the process of fabrication of a computerized machine that eases the process of making rotis without much human intervention.

The flour invented by Avinassh is tested in FSSAI accredited laboratory and got certified for its gluten free content. Avinashh has been selected, incubated and poised to receive funding to further extend its food business

Avinashh is one of the applicants to receive grant-in-aid from Indian Institute of Millet Research(IIMR) of Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) New Delhi.

Founder Pragnanand has several recognitions and awards from Government of India (Department of Ayush) and NGO Tharuni Mitra.



Our Philosophy

“Earth provides enough to satisfy every man's needs but not every man's greed”-Mahatma Gandhi

We at Avinashh strongly believe that Food is Medicine. We believe in Life, sustainability and healthy choices. We believe in the concept of Vocal for Local and adopting renewable practices to reduce carbon footprint on this Earth.

We believe in the welfare of farmers. Working towards security of farmers, we supply quality seeds to tribal farmers to grow millets and directly procure from them offering a premium price when compared to market. In order to transport procured products we use solar/battery operated vehicles to play a part to reduce the carbon footprint.

For our packaging needs, we use locally sourced natural products such as palm leaves, bamboo baskets, cloth/jute bags. We try to eliminate the use of plastic to package our products thus contributing to sustainability.

Our innovation and sustainable practices we follow have framed our philosophy to facilitate, support and guide people with food, diet and life style choices. We believe that the choices that we make today help us to transform this planet a healthy one.

Why our products are unique?

Have you ever tried rolling out flat bread with any of the millet flours available in the market. Due to lack of gluten in millet flours it becomes difficult to shape rotis. At this juncture, flours made by Avinashh offers a solution to you. One can easily roll flat bread with millet flours invented by Avinashh without much effort. Avinashh flours are gluten free and are devoid of simple carbohydrates. Our flour composition consists of 90% of flour and 10% of vegetable herbs which anyone can consume without any guilt.
